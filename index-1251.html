📲 Install ZMONIE App

Follow these 2 steps to install ZMONIE Market on your iPhone/iPad:

1
Tap the Share button
Tap the Share icon (box with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
2
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home Screen" then tap Add

⚠️ Must use Safari browser on iOS for this to work
